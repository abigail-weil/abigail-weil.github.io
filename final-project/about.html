OUR HISTORY

Fremont Vintage Mall (formerly Fremont Antique Mall) opened its doors in 1990 and has become a Fremont Landmark ever since. We have a well established loyal customer base which includes designers, wholesalers, and end users. Our atmosphere is fun and funky. We have an eclectic mix of merchandise that spans the decades.

The painting to the left a framed piece of Fremont History, the Red Door! The Red Door is in The Fremont Vintage Mall's old location before we moved (and is sadly now closed).
